Our Story

In 2010 Co-Founders Sara and Rowan rushed to Devon having been told that Sara’s mother - known to the family as ‘Wellie’ - was having life-saving brain surgery. Whilst she unexpectedly survived her stroke she required 24-hour care and moved into the family home, joining her husband and Sara’s father - from whom she had been separated for over two decades.

It quickly became evident that the home care sector was archaic and desperately in need of change; every attempt Sara made to ensure her parents were cared for in an anxiety-free manner was met with difficulties, inefficient processes and constraints.

She found that two carers from each of three different agencies were well-matched to ‘Wellie’ and ‘Gramps’, but no way existed for these carers to work together in the required 6-person team. Whichever agency she chose, she knew two thirds of the carers would not be suitable.

Rather than settle for this, she convinced these 6 individuals to register with a new agency, which agreed to allocate them entirely to her parents and allowed her to manage the care package. Her mother went on to regain 80% of the functionality her doctors said she would never regain.

This inspired Rowan to leave his job in the City to assemble a team of healthcare professionals, engineers and entrepreneurs so that he could build a platform which offers personalised care to everyone.

Our mission is to make it easy for people to make their own care choices and access the most trusted home care in the world. To join us on our journey, get started today!

Meet the founders

Rowan
Chief Executive Officer

Rowan watched his grandparents receive home care for years. After a year in the City he left his job to build a care system that cares for all elderly people with the compassion they deserve. In his spare time he volunteers for South London Cares.

Neil
Chief Technology Officer

Neil went from living with a huge family in India, to a relative living alone in London. Having someone around who cares made a massive difference to her mood. He wants everyone to have such companionship. He has previously developed software for Google, Microsoft and Morgan Stanley.

Sara
Chief Care Officer

Having spent 35 years in various healthcare roles, Sara has an unparalleled appreciation for standards. After continuously witnessing those without a voice receive sub-standard care she vowed to make it easy to find quality. In her spare time she likes to fight other people's battles for them.
