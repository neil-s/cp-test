Trust

It can be hard for older adults to accept they need help. We also know how exhausting it can be for family members to always be there.

Having a caregiver should be no more complicated than spending time with a friend, why is why we place a very high emphasis on trust. We feel that the key to building trust lies in three areas:

  1. Unbeatable Screening Process

    We have stringent criteria and only accept the very best caregivers into our network.

  2. Carefully Considered Matching

    Our technology carefully matches you to your perfect caregiver. Your CarePledge account also enables you to communicate with them easily, even from afar.

  3. Understanding of Caregivers

    Watch intro videos of our caregivers and browse their profile to see skills, previous experience, specialist knowledge, qualifications and ratings they receive from other clients. All before you have to choose whether you want to meet them.
